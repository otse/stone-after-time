## The Premise

Seyda Neen is gone.

The Bitter Coast has collapsed into rock and swamp.

Time has eaten the world.

But one mine remains intact.

Inside it lives the last dwarf.

He talks.

You listen.

Nothing else happens.

---

## The Cave

A converted kwama mine.

Barrels. Skooma crates.

Ore carts.

Pillows and tea in a strange corner.

Candles.

Poems.

The cave is not luxurious.

But it is lived in.

---

## Yagrum

Not a god.

Not immortal.

Not honest.

He talks like someone who understands the universe slightly too well.

Sometimes he sounds like Jack Black if he read philosophy.

He never directly explains what he did.

---

## Themes

- time decay
- memory erosion
- quiet guilt
- the end of the world
- comfortable ruins
